# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Residents and investors in Bangalore looking for properties for various purposes. They are in a high-stakes decision-making context, seeking reliable data to find the best value for their money.

## Product Purpose
To provide a data-driven real estate analytics platform that simplifies the process of finding, comparing, and predicting house prices in Bangalore. Success is defined by users gaining the confidence to make informed investment decisions.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Friendly, Premium. The interface should feel elite and high-end but remain approachable and easy to navigate. It should evoke trust through precision and "expert confidence."

## Anti-references
Explicitly avoid "AI-generated" aesthetics: no neon-on-black schemes, no generic purple gradients, and no over-designed "cyber" elements. Avoid cluttered or overstimulating layouts that feel "cheap" or "unprofessional."

## Design Principles
- **Expert Confidence**: Use precise data visualization and clean typography to establish authority.
- **Breathable Premium**: Prioritize whitespace and high-quality spatial composition over dense information grids.
- **Value-First Hierarchy**: Ensure the "best value" metrics (Price/Sq.Ft, ROI) are always the most prominent.
- **Intentional Motion**: Use subtle, exponential transitions to guide the eye without being distracting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability.
- Clear font scaling for various screen sizes.
- Support for users who prefer reduced motion by ensuring core functionality remains static-first.
