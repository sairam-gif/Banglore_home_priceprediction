# Design

## Theme
Light Mode. Optimized for clarity and expert confidence in a professional market-analysis context.

## Color Palette
Using OKLCH for perceptual uniformity and premium depth.
- **Brand Primary**: `oklch(60% 0.15 250)` (Elite Blue) - A deep, trustworthy blue inspired by premium real estate platforms.
- **Surface**: `oklch(98% 0.005 250)` (Off-White) - A soft, breathable background to reduce cognitive load.
- **On-Surface**: `oklch(20% 0.02 250)` (Slate) - High-contrast text for expert precision.
- **Accent**: `oklch(70% 0.15 150)` (Emerald) - Used sparingly (≤10%) for "best value" and positive growth indicators.

## Typography
- **Display**: "Geist", sans-serif. Used for headers and primary metrics to convey modern authority.
- **Body**: "Inter", sans-serif. Used for all reading text and data tables for maximum legibility.
- **Scale**: Minor Third (1.20) for a refined, professional hierarchy.

## Spatial Composition
- **Layout**: Bento-grid approach for analytics, ensuring clear data separation.
- **Spacing**: Generous negative space to maintain a "Breathable Premium" feel.
- **Radius**: `24px` for cards and inputs to soften the professional edge and feel "friendly."

## Motion
- **Style**: Subtle, exponential easing (`ease-out-expo`).
- **Energy**: Low energy, high precision. No bounces or decorative distractions.
